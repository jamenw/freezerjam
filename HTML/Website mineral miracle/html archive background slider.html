    How best to use Mineral Miracle?

    Plants, Tomatoes, Strawberries, Roses & Flowers

    Method One: Simply apply 1/2 to 1 tablespoon (scoop included) around each plant every 1 to 4 weeks depending how depleted the soil is and condition of the plant. This method works best with loose soils. Rake and water into soil.

    Method Two: Apply 1/4 cup around each plant when planting. This method may work better with dense soils. Rake and water into soil.

    Compost, humus, manures, or other fertilizers should be added to provide the needed nitrogen, phosphorus and potassium.

    Shrubs, Raspberries & Grapes Apply 1 cup in the spring and in the fall. Add more for larger shrubs. Rake and water into soil.

    Trees Apply 2 to 4 cups in the spring and in the fall. Add more for larger trees. Rake and water into soil.

    Mineral Miracle will not harm or burn plants and is completely safe to use with other fertilizers.
